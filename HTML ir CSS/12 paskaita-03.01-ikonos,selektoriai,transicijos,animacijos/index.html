Norint naudoti ikonas ,reikia prijungti viena is daugelio ikonu biblioteku prie savo svetaines. Galimos bibliotekos;FontAwersome,Bootstrap,MUI,Ant-Design,Tailwind, Bulma ... ICONS REFERENCE/BOOTSTRAP ICONS
